FAQ

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking by logging into your account, going to “My Bookings,” and selecting the cancellation option. Be sure to review the cancellation policy, as some bookings may be non-refundable or have specific terms.

Are the tours guided?

Yes, most of our tours are fully guided by licensed professionals. You’ll find details about the guide, language, and itinerary on each tour’s page.

Can I customize my package?

Absolutely! Many of our travel packages can be tailored to your preferences, including hotel upgrades, additional excursions, and extended stays. Just click on "Customize" during checkout or contact our support team.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not included by default but can be added during the booking process. We highly recommend adding it to protect your trip from unexpected changes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and selected local payment options depending on your region.
